Jio Friends

Jio Friends allows you to always be aware of the whereabouts of everyone in your private circle. Track, locate and get directions to your friends and family. Basically, never again ask the question ‘where are you’.

With real-time tracking you can tell the exact location of where everyone is and get updates as frequently as you wish. All you need to do is put a pugmark (trail) on them. You can even receive alerts each time your loved ones arrive or leave from a place – like school, work or home.
