Executive - Corporate Services (10340)

Job role : Executive

The job

Monitor all activities with respect to Facilities Management and ensure that they are conducted in a timely and accurate manner and in compliance with company policy guidelines on the same resulting in improved functionality and optimum cost effectiveness for the organization.

The candidate will be reporting to Regional Corporate services Head.

Save job Print
Responsibilities
  • Monitor all activities related to Facilities Management to ensure cost effectiveness and adherence to service standards
  • Monitor following activities on a continuous basis and ensure smooth and timely conduct of the same:
    1. a. Office Services
    2. Housekeeping including Pest control
    3. Food court management
    4. Travel Management
    5. Dispatch management
  • Ensure effective supervision of all third-party staff engaged in Facilities Management related activities and escalate discrepancies, if any
  • Ensure timely and accurate raising of requisition with respect to engagement of third-party vendor for various processes
  • Completion of GRN and service entry
  • Ensure compliance to all applicable regulatory statutory requirements with respect to Facilities Management related activities
  • Monitor preparation and submission of required MIS on a periodic basis or as requested by the Management to ensure timeliness and accuracy of the same
  • Respond to and resolve queries in case of any issues, where escalated
  • Ensure cost effectiveness and adherence to service standards in all Facilities Management related activities
  • Ensure availability of office related items like stationary and procurement as per guidelines
  • Ensure smooth movement of People, Material
  • Drive effective relationship management with internal / external stakeholders
  • Manage relationships with internal entities like Commercial Department, AP, Business Group etc. on a continuous basis
  • Coordinate with and provide recommendation to the Commercial Department for the selection and engagement of third-party vendors for various services
  • Respond to and resolve all issues of exceptions and escalate the matter to relevant parties for further probing and action
  • To ensure compliance to internal service standards and guidelines in all the hospitality management related processes
  • To ensure reduction in cost of operations in all processes related to hospitality management
  • To ensure timely payment to all third-party vendors
  • To ensure timely and accurate conduct of all activities related to Facilities Management
Who can apply
  • Graduate in any discipline with experience of 7-10 Years.
  • Knowledge of RIL/RJIL group organization, products and services
  • Knowledge of ERP system deployed at RIL/RJIL group
  • Knowledge of vendors available for various Facilities Management related activities like housekeeping, horticulture etc. along with the cost of services
  • Knowledge of third-party contracts and related rules and regulations
  • Should have good communication, decision making skills
