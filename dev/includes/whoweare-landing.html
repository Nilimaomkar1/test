Our Digital Presence

Digital is a strategic requirement for a nation's progress and Jio is strategically placed to play a significant role in the market for digital services in the country. We would address the entire value chain across various digital services in key domains such as, education, healthcare, security, financial services, government-citizen interfaces and entertainment.

We hold the largest quantum of liberalized spectrum in the country with 2,300 MHz spectrum pan India and 1,800 MHz spectrum in 14 telecom circles. This would enable seamless 4G services through an integrated ecosystem.
